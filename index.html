Olivia is a multidisciplinary artist and designer driven by a passion for creating bold, impactful visual experiences. With an International Baccalaureate Diploma in Visual Arts, she enjoys integrating traditional techniques into her predominantly digital practice. Her work spans independent filmmaking, photography, installation, both print and web-based graphic design. Olivia is currently pursuing a BA in Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague.
