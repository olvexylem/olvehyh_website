Olivia Huynh (b.2004, Sydney) is an Interdisciplinary Designer inspired by bold imagery and atmospheric spatial design. With a background in Illustration, Product Design, and a BA Degree in Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art (The Hague), her practice intertwines digital technology and precise craftsmanship to invent unique, innovative forms of visual expression.
