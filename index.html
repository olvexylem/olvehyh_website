Olivia (b.2004, Sydney) is an interdisciplinary designer. Her work focuses on image, moving-image / film, and object-space. With a background in Visual Arts and Product Design in her formative years of school (SG), she deeply values abstract expression and precise craftsmanship. Now finishing her Bachelor of Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art (NL), Olivia’s contemporary design practice intertwines digital tools with industrial craftsmanship.
