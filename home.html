Born in Sydney and raised in Singapore, Olivia is a multidisciplinary artist and designer driven by an undying passion to create bold, impactful visual experiences. With an International Baccalaureate Diploma in Visual Art, she enjoys integrating traditional techniques into her predominantly digital practice. Olivia is currently pursing a BA in Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.
