Olivia Huynh is an interdisciplinary visual designer.

Her work spans image / moving-image making, editorial publications, web development, installation, and beyond. Earning her International Baccalaureate Diploma in Visual Arts, she enjoys maneuvering craftwork with precision, and continues to interweave manual techniques into her predominantly digital practice, giving life to contemporary visions. Olivia currently pursues a BA in Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague (NL).
