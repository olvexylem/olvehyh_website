« Acid »
A painting inspired by the acid-trip. The idea of profound epiphanies and revelations characterises a form of growth that helps one overcome fears and insecurities. The ability to unsee weakness and vulnerabilities as a symptom of deficiency helps us accept and fully embrace our unique individuality. Staying true to one's self is an ultimate strength.
Oil on Canvas
1188 ✕ 841 mm
