Human Routine is a video project depicting a visual language about the human routine, highlighting a profound beauty to the common monotony that illustrates our lives.

Human Routine Posters display the varying sequences of four core human activities. (1) Human Routine final poster, A3 laserprint. (2) Human Routine experimental poster, A3 risograph print. (3) Human Routine experimental poster no.2, A1 silkscreen print.
