Acid

The visual concept of this painting arose from personal experiences with sudden epiphanies and revelations that made my fear and insecurities discipate. To unsee weakness and vulnerability as symptoms of deficiency is an attitude that can strengthen and embrace humanity in all our individuality.


Oil on Canvas
1188 × 841 mm
Singapore, March 2022
