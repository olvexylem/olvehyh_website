Cloud

A modular typeface inspired by explosion clouds. Whereby each letter of the uppercase latin alphabet can be constructed by removing different components of a hand-illustrated, 16-component explosion cloud.


Software — Glyphs 3
Material (puzzle) — Birch Wood

Typeface · Illustration · Object · CAD
